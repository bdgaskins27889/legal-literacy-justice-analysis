This Study Is a Foundation, Not a Final Word

Current Limitations
01Federal vs. State Scope
Focused on federal data; state courts (where most cases occur) may show different patterns.
02Proxy Variables
Representation type is a proxy and doesn’t capture individual attorney resources or experience.
03Observational Nature
Unobserved factors (e.g., evidence strength) limit strict causal claims despite controls.
Future Research
01State-Level Replication
Apply models to state systems to test whether geographic bias reduction holds locally.
02NLP Analysis of Transcripts
Use NLP on transcripts to detect linguistic biases missed by structured data.
03Longitudinal Impact
Track recidivism to assess whether fairer sentencing yields better long‑term outcomes.
