Data-Driven Evidence Supports Three Actionable Policy Reforms

01
Mandatory Fairness Audits
Require all predictive algorithms used in sentencing to pass the "80% Rule" for disparate impact before deployment. Tools that fail—like the standard logistic model—must be rejected or recalibrated.
Target: Algorithmic Accountability
02
Context-Aware Sentencing
Include district-level context so judges can separate individual culpability from systemic geographic severity.
Target: Judicial Consistency
03
Public Defense Parity
Address the "Representation Gap" by tying public defender funding to prosecution budgets. Reducing the resource disparity is the most direct way to sever the link between race, poverty, and outcome.
Target: Procedural Justice
