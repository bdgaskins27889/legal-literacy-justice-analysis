Anticipated Questions & Discussion Points

Q1 Data Bias
If historical data is biased, will the model repeat that bias?
Mitigation: fairness constraints, remove direct race features.
Q2 Causality
Does attorney type cause outcomes or act as a proxy?
Controlled for offense level and history.
Q3 Generalizability
Are federal findings applicable to state/local courts?
Limitations: may underestimate state-level effects.
Q4 Metric Selection
Why prioritize the 80% rule over accuracy?
See fairness trade-off discussion.
Q5 Implementation
Is the multilevel model feasible for courts?
Modern compute power makes it feasible.
Q6 Ethics
Should algorithms play any role in sentencing?
Recommendation: keep human-in-the-loop.
