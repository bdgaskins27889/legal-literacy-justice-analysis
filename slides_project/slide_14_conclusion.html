The Scales of Justice Can Be Recalibrated

Data science is not just a mirror reflecting the system's flaws—it is a lever for correcting them.
Acknowledge Bias
We cannot fix what we do not measure. Rigorous auditing is the first step toward justice.
Design for Fairness
Fairness must be an architectural input, not an afterthought. Models must be built to serve justice, not just accuracy.
Empower Reform
Use data to arm public defenders and policymakers with the evidence needed to dismantle systemic inequities.
